Lumas Ray video

A video AI model for generating realistic and fantastical videos from text prompts and images.

Dream Machine is a video generation model built on a scalable, multimodal transformer architecture. It generates physically accurate and consistent videos directly from text instructions and images.

Key Features

  • Direct video generation
  • Handles both realistic and fantastical scenes
  • Maintains physical accuracy and consistency
  • Supports text and image inputs
  • Built for efficiency and scalability

Architecture Goals

Dream Machine is designed to be:

  • Fast and responsive
  • Highly expressive
  • Capable of handling complex scenes
